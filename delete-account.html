Delete Your Account and Data

My State Scheduler gives you full control over your account. Here is how to permanently delete your account and personal data, and exactly what happens when you do.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your My State Scheduler account and all of your data at any time, directly from the app:

1

Open the My State Scheduler app and sign in to your account.

2

Go to the Account tab.

3

Tap "Delete account."

4

Type your account email address to confirm.

5

Tap Delete.

Deletion is immediate and permanent.

There is no grace period and no way to recover your account or data once you confirm the deletion.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your name, date of birth, and the last 4 digits of your Social Security number
  • Your Texas driver's license or ID number
  • Your phone number and email address
  • Your account login credentials — you are signed out of every device
  • Your booked appointments
  • Your Auto-Searches

If you have an active, paid Auto-Search running, it is cancelled and refunded according to our standard refund policy as part of deleting your account. See our Terms of Service for the refund policy.

What We Keep

A very small amount of information is retained after your account is deleted, with no personal data attached:

  • An anonymized payment record — the payment processor's transaction ID and amount, with no personal information attached, kept for accounting, tax, and legal purposes.
  • Shared applicant information — if you booked an appointment on behalf of someone else (for example, a family member) and that person's exact information is also actively used by another My State Scheduler account, we keep that person's record only for as long as another account is still using it. Once no account references it, it is deleted too.

Can't Access the App?

If you have uninstalled the app or can't sign in, you can still request deletion of your account and data. Email us at support@mystatescheduler.com from the email address on your account, and we will complete the deletion within 30 days.

Contact Us

Questions about deleting your account or data? Reach out:

Email: support@mystatescheduler.com

We aim to respond within 5 business days and complete account-deletion requests within 30 days.
