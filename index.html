Every Auto-Search gives you 30 days of searching — 30 continuous calendar days that start when you pay. The clock keeps running whether Auto-Search is actively searching or paused, so paused days count too. You can split those 30 days into as many search ranges as you like, but once 30 days pass from purchase, the Auto-Search expires. Example: if you search for 3 days, pause, then try to activate more 35 days later, your window has already ended — so activate your ranges within the 30-day window.
